=====

Manual method / User managed bkp 
- Cold Backup (spfile / pfile , redo, arch datafile, ct file)
	- shutdown DB and take bkp and start DB --------------- Consistent / No need of recpovery 

- HOT bkp (spfile / pfile , redo, arch datafile, ct file)
	- put DB in begin bkp mode and take bkp and end bkp --- In-Consistent / Need of recpovery 

Automated method
- RMAN 
	- backup database;-------------------------------------- In-Consistent / Need of recpovery

- In-Consistent
